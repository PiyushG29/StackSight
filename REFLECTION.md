# Reflection

This file should be completed with your real week-by-week decisions, debugging history, AI usage notes, and self-assessment. I have not invented personal experience on your behalf.

## 1. The hardest bug you hit this week, and how you debugged it

Document the real bug, the hypotheses you formed, the tests you ran, and the final fix.

## 2. A decision you reversed mid-week, and what made you reverse it

Explain the original direction, what evidence changed your mind, and the resulting trade-off.

## 3. What you would build in week 2 if you had it

Cover expansion ideas like richer benchmarks, external pricing sync, or CRM handoff.

## 4. How you used AI tools

List which AI tools you used, where they helped, where they failed, and one specific incorrect suggestion you caught.

## 5. Self-rating

Give 1–10 scores for discipline, code quality, design sense, problem-solving, and entrepreneurial thinking with one sentence each.
