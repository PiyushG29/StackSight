# Landing Copy

- Hero headline: Cut AI spend without slowing the team
- Subheadline: Audit your current AI tool stack, see where you are overspending, and get a concrete monthly savings plan in minutes.
- Primary CTA copy: Run my free audit
- Social proof block: Mocked for prototype. “Used by product, engineering, and founder teams trying to control runaway AI software spend.”

## FAQ

### Why is the audit free?

The audit is a lead-generation wedge. It creates value immediately, and high-savings teams may later want help capturing those savings through discounted credits.

### Do I need to create an account?

No. You can run the audit without logging in. Email is only requested after the result appears.

### How do you decide what to recommend?

The engine uses rule-based plan-fit logic, seat counts, pricing references, and use-case matching. It does not ask an LLM to invent the math.

### Will my public report expose my company details?

No. The public page strips identifying lead fields and only shows tool choices plus savings logic.

### What if my stack is already efficient?

The app says so directly. It still offers a way to save the audit and hear about future pricing or optimization changes.
